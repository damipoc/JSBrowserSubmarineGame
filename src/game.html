Submarine Game

by Damian Poclitar

















Setup Stage:
Click on a cell and type a number between 5 and 9, a fuel cell with that amount of fuel will be placed.
Click on a cell and type "o" to place an obstacle, the user and the killer submarine can not go there.
Click on a cell and type "u" to place the User submarine to control, only one allowed.
Click on a cell and type "k" to place a killer submarine to move around, avoid those or else you will lose.
Anything other than that will not be accepted, once you are done press the "Start" button.

Play Stage:
The objective is simple, collect all the fuel cells("5-9") without getting caught by the killer submarines("k").
The scoreboard above shows you the current round, current fuel, your score and the killer submarines score.
You can only move if you have fuel, collect the fuel cells to replenish your fuel and add points to your score.
If the killer robots take a fuel cell then they get the points, they do not need fuel to move so they have unlimited moves.

Movement:
w - Up
a - Left
d - Right
x - Down
Enter one of these letters in the field bellow the board and click "Submit" to move.

If you manage to collect all the fuel cells or get have a higher score than the killer submarines without getting killed you win!
If the game starts without any killer submarines you also win.
If the killer submarines manage to get a higher score than you or kill your submarine then you lose.
Else the game will result in a draw.
